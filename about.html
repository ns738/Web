About Us

We are the local authority for the City of Westminster in London. Our work supports residents, businesses and visitors by ensuring strong local democracy, transparent decision making and effective community services.

British values guide how we serve our community. They help maintain a fair, respectful and inclusive society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Core British Values

  • Democracy Encouraging participation and ensuring every voice can be heard.
  • The Rule of Law Promoting fairness, safety and respect for civil law.
  • Individual Liberty Supporting personal freedom and protecting the vulnerable.
  • Mutual Respect & Tolerance Celebrating Westminster's diversity and fostering understanding.

These values shape our policies, our services and our partnerships across the community. By upholding them, we aim to create a Westminster that is safe, welcoming and supportive for all.
